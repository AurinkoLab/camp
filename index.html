System Overview — What Kids Will Experience

Join us for an exciting summer filled with robots and technology! Our goal is to create a supportive environment where every child (ages 7–14) can grow, learn, and master modern engineering.

At AurinkoLab, we use hands-on learning in small groups (8–14 participants) to ensure personalized attention for every child. A short placement test on Day 1 helps us form balanced groups so each child learns at their optimal pace.

Programming & Robotics

Learn programming fundamentals with Scratch and Python while building real robots with professional safety equipment.

Solar Karts

Build and ride Solar Electric Karts. Master the physics of speed, energy, and sustainable engineering.

Solar Boats

Build and test solar-powered boats, learning about renewable energy and marine engineering principles.

Fun & Friends

Enjoy warm meals, make new friends, and explore technology in a cozy historic space built for curious minds.

Student Projects Showcase

See what our amazing students have built in previous camps — from solar-powered vehicles to innovative robotics projects!

Solar-Powered Go-Kart

Students design and build their own solar-powered vehicles, learning about renewable energy and mechanical engineering.

DIY Solar Boats

Young engineers create solar-powered boats, testing their designs on water while learning about buoyancy and solar energy.

Professional Lab Work

Students work with real engineering equipment and safety protocols, preparing them for future STEM careers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do children need prior experience?

No prior experience is needed! Our curriculum is designed for all skill levels, and our placement test helps us group children appropriately so everyone is challenged at the right level.

What should my child bring?

Children should bring a water bottle, comfortable clothes, and closed-toe shoes. All technical equipment and materials are provided.

Are there any dietary accommodations?

Yes, we can accommodate most dietary restrictions. Please note any special requirements during registration.

What safety measures are in place?

All activities are supervised by trained staff. Safety equipment is provided for all technical activities, and our staff-to-child ratio ensures proper supervision at all times.

What if my child needs to miss a day?

Our curriculum is designed to build day by day, but our instructors will help your child catch up if they miss a session.
